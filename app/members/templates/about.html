Mission Statement

With the rapid expansion of technology, individuals have become more and more disconnected with physical society. Eureka aims to reconnect users with their local community in a gamefied application that promotes them to visit "Eurekas!" or local businneses, parks, events, and more. Through the pet and leaderboard system, users are motivated to compete against their friends and other locals to visit the most amount of "Eurekas" each month, supporting local community and promoting economy.
